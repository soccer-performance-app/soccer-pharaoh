FAQ

Common questions.

Is this legit?
Yes. The program is generated by an AI trained on real sports science and position-specific training principles. It's not a template — every section is written specifically for your position, schedule, and goals. Players at amateur, semi-pro, and youth academy level have used it.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your profile page in one tap and you won't be charged again. No hoops, no emails, no waiting.
What if I'm not on a team?
The program adapts. If you have no match days or practice sessions, it builds entirely around solo training and gym work. You'll still get a complete program — just structured around what you actually have.
What if I have no space?
Tell us your space when you fill the form — backyard, indoors, half field, or full pitch. Every drill and session is scaled to fit. Indoors gets bodyweight, tight-space ball work, and mobility. Nothing gets skipped, just adapted.
